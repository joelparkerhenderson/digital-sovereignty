# Digital Sovereignty Checklist for Public Sector Organizations

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2025-11-20  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive assessment and implementation checklist for achieving digital sovereignty in government and public sector organizations

---

## Executive Summary

This checklist enables public sector organizations to assess, plan, and implement digital sovereignty measures. Use this as a living document to track progress toward digital independence, data protection, and technological autonomy.

**Critical Context:**
- 79% of government agencies are reviewing technology strategy due to digital sovereignty concerns
- 40% of Asia/Pacific government agencies are using or planning sovereign cloud by 2025
- SecNumCloud certification is becoming standard for sensitive public sector workloads
- The EU Data Act (September 2025) mandates data portability and interoperability
